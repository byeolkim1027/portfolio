It’s your PARADISE

Let's introduce Paradise Hotel Busan.
Paradise Hotel Busan is a special first-class (5-star) hotel with a total of 532 rooms affiliated with Paradise Group, and has been loved as a representative domestic brand hotel since its opening in 1981.

Paradise Hotel Busan is always willing to make bold facility investments for customer's ease. Paradise Hotel Busan introduced design management to its service in 2007 and is always pursuing new trends, including adding design content to invisible service products.

Green ‘‘PARADISE’’

  • Investment in installing high-efficiency boilers reduces CO2 Paradise Hotel Busan has been investing 560 million won since 2003 to replace its hotel facilities with high-efficiency boiler facilities called inverter condensing boilers, reducing energy costs and dramatically reducing CO₂ generation under international climate agreements.
  • Eco-friendly LED lighting system To reduce carbon emissions by lighting, LED lights were first installed around the banquet hall, including the grand banquet hall, the capri room, and the Sicily room. After that, we plan to expand LED lighting for the global environment by installing LED lights in separate rooms of buffet restaurants, sculpture fountains in the main building, and lobby lounges.
  • Investment in Hotel Septic Tank Waste Water Treatment Facilities In preparation for the sewage and wastewater generated by the hotel in February 2010, we invested a total of 160 million won in the construction of a discharge pump and pipe that directly connects the sewage treatment plant in eastern Busan. Paradise Hotel Busan is always looking for a systematic and efficient way to operate septic tank facilities.
